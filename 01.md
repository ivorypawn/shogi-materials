(Quote from [Tomohide Kawasaki, *Japanese-English Shogi Dictionary*, Nekomado, 2013](https://store.shopping.yahoo.co.jp/nekomadoshop/h047.html))

### ã²ã£ã— ã€å¿…è‡³/å¿…æ­»ã€‘ *n. Brinkmate.*
